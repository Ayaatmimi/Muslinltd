About Us

Export & Import Logistic Business

Meet Muslin International Limited, a private limited company registered with RJSC in Bangladesh in 2021. With a partner company named Cotton Muslin International established in 2015. The Muslin International Limited started its business more than five years ago, and its business operations recently spread out to China, India, Dubai, and Bangladesh. The company does imports from Hong Kong, China, India, UK, USA, Europe, and some other African countries. The Imported product ranges are fabrics, tiles, cycles, mobile accessories, computers, computer accessories, electrical goods, car accessories, medical equipment, motor vehicles spare parts, heavy industrial machinery, generator, agriculture equipment, etc. The Muslin International Limited exports various types of readymade garments from Bangladesh to different countries around the world. The company specialized in import, trading, and export business.
